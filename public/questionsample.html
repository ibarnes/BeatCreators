Writing your question

Make your question short and clear. State it as a question.

Good:

  • “What is the best way to market music on Myspace?”
  • “What websites are good resources for equipment?”
  • “How do I negotiate my royalties?”

Bad:

  • “Looking for some help with some stuff?”
  • “How do I make music, where can I get equipment, and what are the advantages and disadvantages of a record deal?”
  • “What are the best websites for musicians?”

Adding details (Be descriptive and concise):

  • Be descriptive:
    Include the details an expert will need to answer your question. Ask yourself: if I were to ask this question, what data would they want before answering?
  • Be concise:
    Keep your details short and to-the-point. Experts will appreciate it if you don’t give them too much to read!
